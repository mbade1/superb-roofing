About Us

We are a family owned and operated roofing contractor serving south Texas. Our father started this business in 1989 with big dreams of growing it into a prosperous career. We have worked hard and diligently to carry out that dream and have carried it down 3 generations!

We provide our customers with top quality workmanship, materials, and overall service. Our team is knowledgeable in all different roofing systems and what they require. We also provide storm restoration and work for insurance proceeds on all roofs that are qualified. We take care of the insurance process for you, offer free inspections and estimates, and ensure the upmost reliability of our company.

A roof is one of the most important features of a home or business. It provides shelter and protection. We promise to always be there to keep that roof over your head in the best shape possible. We take pride in our company, in our men, and in our work.

We work for your family and ours! That is the Superb way.
